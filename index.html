School History
📖 Our Journey

Tahira Memorial Public School was established with a vision to provide quality education to the children of Bhelahi, Mahishi, and surrounding areas of Saharsa district, Bihar.

🎯 Mission

To provide holistic education that develops academic excellence, moral values, leadership qualities, and a passion for lifelong learning in every child.

✨ Vision

To create a learning environment where every student realizes their potential and becomes a responsible, compassionate, and contributing member of society.

🏆 Key Milestones
  • Established with Classes 1-5 (Primary Section)
  • Expanded to Classes 6-8 (Secondary Section)
  • Over 450+ students enrolled
  • Dedicated faculty of 15+ teachers
  • State-of-the-art facilities including labs and library
  • Active participation in sports, culture, and academics
💡 Commitment to Excellence

We believe in nurturing not just academicians, but well-rounded individuals who can think critically, act responsibly, and lead with integrity. Our commitment extends to the development of each child's unique talents and capabilities.
